About Appointment Manager

Appointment Manager is a simple and efficient web application designed to help individuals and businesses manage their appointments with ease. Whether you runs a hospital, clinic, or consultancy, our system helps you schedule, organize, and track all your bookings in one place.

With a clean interface and useful features, Appointment Manager reduces missed appointments and saves time.

  • Login & Signup: Users can create accounts and log in safely with passwords.
  • Quick Appointment Booking: Book appointments easily using a simple form with date, time, and add message.
  • Automatic Notifications: We receive confirmation messages after booking appointments.
  • Database Integration: All appointments and user data are stored securely in MySQL for easy retrieval.
